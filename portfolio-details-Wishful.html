WishfulBazaar — Sales & Customer Behavior Analytics

An end-to-end analytics project to understand e-commerce performance and customer behavior across the full funnel. The dashboard tracks Sales Success (confirmed orders without return), Abandoned Sales (unconfirmed orders), Return Sales, Profit, session duration, and age segments—built on joined Order, Customer, and Campaign data for a unified view of the business.

Key insights: Sales success dominates overall revenue, but abandoned sales remain significant; COD is still the most used payment method despite rising digital payments; customers aged 20–30 drive the most transactions; Electronics and Fashion lead as best-selling categories; YoY profit growth has slowed since 2022 even as cumulative profit increased; top-performing countries include Canada and Australia; and monthly income shows no clear correlation with average monthly purchases.

Recommendations: Fix checkout bottlenecks (speed, login, payment UX) and add “complete your purchase” reminders; incentivize digital payments (e-wallet/card promos); concentrate campaigns on the 20–30 age segment and top categories; prioritize high-margin products to counter profit slowdown; localize campaigns and fulfillment for top countries; and segment customers by purchase frequency rather than income to grow loyalty and repeat buys.
